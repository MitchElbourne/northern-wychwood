About Us

Nothern Wychwood Limited is a licenced corporate and trust service provider located on the Isle of Man.

Focussed on building relationships with clients based mainly in sub-Saharan Africa, NWL provides secure asset protection structures. The company's culture is based on a strong family friendly and caring environment, with a focus on building long-lasting relationships with clients.

Northern Wychwood Limited was formed in 2008 as a result of the merger between Northern Limited and Wychwood Trust Limited.

Susanne and Simon Fearnhead became executive directors of the company on 2 March 2017 and sole shareholders of Northern Wychwood Limited on 1 May 2018. Bryan and Whitney formally left the company on 30 April 2018, but continue to be available and advise clients as necessary.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to be the preferred partner for African clients wanting to reach out globally, entrepreneurs, HNWs and companies that have built or are building cross-border businesses mainly in emerging markets, especially Africa.

Our focus is to retain our clients and provide value to their structures from our core competencies.

We work closely with a number of trusted advisors to provide bespoke legal, international tax and structuring solutions.
