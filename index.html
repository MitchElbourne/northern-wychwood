who we are

Northern Wychwood Limited is a licenced corporate and trust service provider located  on the Isle of Man.

Focussed on building long lasting relationships with our clients, NWL provides secure asset protection structures. The company's culture is based on a strong family friendly and caring environment, with a focus on building long-lasting relationships with clients.

Northern Wychwood Limited was formed in 2008 as a result of the merger between Northern Limited and Wychwood Trust Limited.

Susanne and Simon Fearnhead became executive directors of the company on 2 March 2017 and sole shareholders of Northern Wychwood Limited on 1 May 2018. The previous shareholders / directors formally left the company on 30 April 2018, but continue to be available and advise clients as necessary.

NWL has an international outlook and a global reach to achieve the most up to date and secure structures for our clients.
